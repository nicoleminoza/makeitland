About

Make It Land Studio is the practice of Nicole Miñoza.

For two decades I worked on the most overlooked thing in software: typography. Everyone used it; almost no one thought it mattered. I turned it from a finishing touch into a reason people chose Adobe and stayed.

That is the method. Find the advantage a business has learned to overlook, make it measurable, and design everything around it until it becomes the reason you get picked. Strategy, product, brand, and craft usually live in different people. In me they live in one, now with AI that takes me from thinking to shipped in weeks, not quarters. The judgment stays human. The details are the whole thing.

Make It Land brings that method to a small number of founders and leaders who are undervalued and ready to be chosen. Find the one thing. Prove it. Make it land.
