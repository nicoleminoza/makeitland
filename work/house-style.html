The method

Every prompt is written in one house style, extracted from real work and documented so the taste is repeatable:

—A sharp, credentialed persona with an attitude.
—Legislate the language: banned-word lists, no AI-isms.
—Every claim carries a number or a name.
—Lead with ownership verbs, dictate the output order.
—Calibrate with bad-to-good examples, and build in an acceptance test.
