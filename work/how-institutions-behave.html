How Institutions Behave

Brand is behavior, not image. A close read of how nine landmark institutions earned trust by performing their values, and the mechanics any brand can borrow.

Experience the thesis ->
The argument

A logo change is not a rebrand. Institutions earn trust through how they behave over time, not the mark they put on it.

The work

An interactive piece benchmarking nine landmark cultural-institution rebrands against that standard. Strategy, writing, design, and build, all solo, in one piece.

Why it matters here

The same conviction that runs the studio: positioning is proven by behavior, not asserted. Here it is, made into something you can move through.
